Content Types

The hub is agnostic about what sorts of content is in it, so you must describe what sort content it can expect. You may install as many types as content you accept, and then you begin the process of associating the schemes you have put in the hub to the content type. For example, a scholarly published article is likely to bear information in the ISSN scheme, since this is a standard naming system for academic journals. To add a new content type, click the link below. Browse types at left.
